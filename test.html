About Me

Career

I am a learner, problem solver, collaborator, encourager, leader, listener, counselor, engineer of learning, developer, facilitator. If you ask me what is a teacher, I would answer all of these things. This is my job. This is what I do. I love my job and the challenge that it brings every day.


I first started out teaching science but began sharing my passion of building, designing and innovating with my students which lead to the creation of my current position, Science Technology Engineering Mathematics (STEM) teacher.

    Schools:

  • Mid-Prairie Middle School as STEM and Science teacher
  • Washington Junior High School as a Science teacher
  • Fulton High School as a Biology and Chemistry teacher
